Welcome

The Beikoku Shidokan hombu (home) dojo is always open to new students and visitors alike. Whether you are interested in joining, you are currently a Beikoku Shidokan member, or you are just interested in observing. Dojo location and class times can be found below.

If you are visiting from out of town, please check Sensei's itinerary before making travel arrangements to ensure he will not be out of town.

Location

The hombu dojo is located at 2018 East Michigan Ave, Lansing, Michigan.

About

Headquarters of the Beikoku Shidokan Association, Sensei Iha opened the hombu dojo in Lansing, MI in 1976.

Try a class

Try a complimentary class! New students are welcome to join at any time. Both youth and adult classes are available. The study of karate-do can become a long-term personal investment, so it is recommended that you observe or participate in a class, talk with the instructors, and then decide if the dojo and style are right for you. Call (517) 482-3217 or email to arrange a time to visit.
