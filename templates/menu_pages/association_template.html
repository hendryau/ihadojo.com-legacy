About

The Beikoku Shido-kan Association encompasses active members training in over 30 affiliated dojos located throughout North America, as well as overseas in Australia, Israel and Russia.

Headquartered in Lansing, Michigan USA, the Association is overseen by Seikichi Iha, Hanshi 10th Dan. Iha Sensei's often-stated goal is the continuous promotion of friendship and cooperation among all students of karatedo. Only through this, can one truly pratice and grow.

To accomplish this goal, Iha Sensei maintains an intensive travel schedule to stay in touch with all of his students throughout the world. For your convenience, his itinerary is maintained on this web site.

In addition to regular seminars held by affiliated dojos, every summer, a National Training Seminar is sponsored by the Association's Hombu dojo in Lansing, Michigan. This must-attend event attracts participants from around the world, and includes many Okinawan Shidokan teachers and accomplished students.

Membership

Your membership in the Beikoku Shido-kan Association signifies your support to Iha Sensei in helping promote friendship and cooperation to all students of karatedo throughout the world. In addition, you'll receive the following member only benefits:

  • Membership certificate
  • Directory of current members
  • Black Belt certification (rank prerequisites and testing fee are still applicable)
  • Discount on association sponsored events
  • Discount on Activewear
