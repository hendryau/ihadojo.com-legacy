National Training Seminar (NTS) 2018
July 20th-22nd, 2018

Join Sensei Seikichi Iha, Hanshi, 10th Dan, and fellow Beikoku Shidokan Association members for a tremendous learning opportunity. Shidokan practitioners and supporters from around the globe will gather in Lansing, Michigan to learn and train together. NTS is a great time to reconnect with old friends and learn together with new people within Shidokan Karate community.

Plan to join Sensei in Lansing July 20th-22nd, 2018.

Registration

Registration is now open.You can visit the registration form on a separate page here.

Who Should Attend?

All Shidokan karate students are encouraged to attend. Seminar sessions are designed so that anyone, regardless of age or rank, can benefit and learn. Throughout the weekend you will be able to train with and share information with fellow students and teachers from around the world.

Non-Shidokan karateka are also welcomed to attend.

Sensei Iha invites you to improve your karate practice through the development of expanded friendships and the cooperation learned by training with new partners.

Location
Lansing Center, Lansing MI, USA

This year, NTS will be held at the Lansing Center. The address is 333 E. Michigan Ave, Lansing, MI 48933, just a few miles from the hombu dojo.

There will be construction on the Eastbound side of the Michigan Avenue Bridge during the seminar. Use the "Stage Two" detour:


The Lansing Center offers:

  • Spacious, air-conditioned training areas on the ground floor of the conference center
  • Changing areas and restrooms adjacent to the seminar rooms
  • Easy walking access to hotel and restaurants during breaks
  • Rooms at a conference flat rate (hotel space is limited, book soon!) When booking a room online, use the conference discount code OOK718 to receive a discounted room rate. When calling to book a room, say you're coming for the National Training Seminar.
  • A Saturday banquet dinner with is included in the "Seminar and Events" conference rate
  • Plenty of close and cheap parking
  • Easy access by hotel shuttle, bus, and freeways to Lansing Capitol City Airport, the hombu dojo, shopping centers, entertainment, Lugnuts Stadium, Michigan's Capitol, and the Michigan State University Campus

Youth Sessions

Youth sessions for ages 7-13 are available Friday-Saturday or Saturday-only and include the youth tournament on Saturday July 21. Teens age 14 and up can sign up for the Friday-Sunday seminar options and participate in the Saturday Youth and Teen Tournament. (For youth registration options, see the registration site here.)

Kobudo

A kobudo break-out session led by Sensei Takafumi Nakayama will focus on bo kata and partnership applications. All learning levels welcome. Please bring your equipment if possible. Only a limited number of bo will be available on site.

Dan Test

The Dan test will be held on Thursday, July 19th at the Lansing Center. Doors will open at 4:30pm for test participants to warm up. Testing Board Members will hold a pre-examination meeting at 5:00pm. The test will begin at 6:00pm.

Cell phones must be turned off during the test.

No photography or video taping is permitted during the test. The test will be recorded and distributed to those testing at a later date.

Downtown Lansing

Visit the NTS Conference visitor page on the Greater Lansing Sports Authority website for info maps and information on things to do and places to eat in the downtown area. The conference center is walking distance to a wide variety of restaurants, Lansing's baseball park, and other attractions.

Nearby bicycle and kayak rentals are available for anyone interested in enjoying the Grand River and river trail during the day.
